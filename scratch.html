All About Suriname

One of earth's most hidden gems

What is Suriname?

Suriname is a small, culturally rich country located on the northeastern coast of South America, bordered by Guyana to the west, French Guiana to the east, Brazil to the south, and the Atlantic Ocean to the north. Formerly known as Dutch Guiana, it is the only country in South America where Dutch is the official language. Much of Suriname is covered by dense tropical rainforest, making it one of the most forested countries in the world and a hotspot for biodiversity.

Population & Cultural Heritage

Its population stands at a mere 640,000 people, with about 240,000 being densely populated along the northern coast, especially in the capital city, Paramaribo, which is known for its colonial architecture and multicultural heritage. Their five main cultural groups consist of Indigenous peoples, those of African descent, Indian, Javanese, and Chinese.


Nature

Suriname is renowned for its rich and largely untouched natural environment, with dense tropical rainforest covering most of the country. It is one of the most forested nations in the world, home to an incredible variety of wildlife, including jaguars, giant river otters, monkeys, colorful birds, and countless plant species. The landscape is shaped by wide rivers, waterfalls, wetlands, and savannas, which are vital to both ecosystems and local communities. Much of Suriname’s interior remains undeveloped and protected, allowing nature to thrive with minimal human impact. This abundance of preserved wilderness makes Suriname an important center for biodiversity and a standout example of natural conservation in South America.

Paradise on Earth

Suriname is an ideal destination for travelers seeking authentic experiences, unspoiled nature, and rich cultural diversity. Vast rainforests stretch as far as the eye can see, filled with waterfalls, winding rivers, and extraordinary wildlife, creating a sense of pure, unspoiled beauty. With much of the country protected and lightly populated, visitors experience a calm, almost timeless environment far removed from modern crowds and stress. The air feels cleaner, the landscapes feel endless, and nature remains the dominant force. Combined with the warmth of its people and the country’s cultural balance and simplicity, Suriname offers a serene, natural paradise that feels both authentic and deeply restorative. So keep it in mind for your next travel destination!
